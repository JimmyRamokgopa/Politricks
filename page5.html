THE DARK AGE OF LOAD SHEDDING

Cartoonist Jimmy Ramokgopa depicts the age of load shedding. This was an unpublished cartoon from 10 years ago, dated 25 January 2008! It is somewhat surprising to note that South Africa is experiencing the same problems as it did over a decade ago. The cartoon has been edited slightly. The original had "Metal Age" instead of "Industrial Age" in the centre - 09 December 2018.
