THE CLIFTON BEACH SHEEP

Cartoonist Jimmy Ramokgopa depicts how things might have been for the sheep prior to being at a Clifton beach and being slaughtered thereafter. This is a commentary on racial relations, access in South Africa and the reaction of Mmusi Maimane as a DA leader and a black man. The protest came about after private security have been targeting black people on the beach and instructing them to leave (allegedly) - 30 December 2018.
